What works today · How to try it

Pay bills and people, straight from a card.

WLTH Pay lets a verified user load money from their card and send it out to the businesses and people they owe. Everything that touches money — identity checks, cards, and payouts — runs on Airwallex behind the scenes. Here's what's built, and a step-by-step way to try it yourself.

Behind the scenes

What Airwallex actually handles

— identity

A real account per user

Your verification creates a connected account at Airwallex. That's what makes you allowed to move money.

— card

Safe card handling

Cards are entered into Airwallex's own secure field and turned into a token. The raw card number never reaches WLTH.

— payout

The actual transfer

When you send, Airwallex moves the funds to your payee's bank — using the verified address on your account.

Try it yourself

Two ways to start

Open the demo, choose the Pay service, then pick a starting point depending on what you want to see.

Option A · fastest

Demo account

Logs you in as a user who's already verified and ready. Skip setup and go straight to adding payees, adding a card, and sending payments.

signed in as · Nikhil Sahani — verified, active account

Option B · full journey

Fresh account

Creates a brand-new user so you can walk through the whole verification flow from scratch before reaching the app.

signed in as · a new user — starts at identity check

Open the demo and choose Pay

Launch the WLTH demo shell, then pick Pay from the services menu (or the “launch Pay” button). Choose Demo account or Fresh account.

Get verified · fresh account only

Fill in your personal details, your address, upload a photo of an ID document, then review and submit. (The Demo account skips this — it's already verified.)

Add someone to pay

Go to Payees → Add payee. Enter a business or person and their bank details (BSB + account) or a PayID.

Add a card

Add a card through the secure Airwallex form. This is the card your payments will draw from.

Make a payment

Hit Make a payment, pick your payee, upload an invoice or type the bill in, choose your card, check the fee breakdown, and Send. Try a batch too by uploading a CSV.

Watch it land

Return to the Dashboard and Payments history to see the payment, its status, and the receipt.

🧪

This runs in a sandbox — no real money moves. Use Airwallex test card details when adding a card, and feel free to send freely; nothing leaves a real bank account.
